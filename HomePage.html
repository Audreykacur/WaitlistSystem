Who is Waitlist for?

Any company who has a long list of customers that request your services that are not able to be added to your schedule

Why was Waitlist created?

A customizable design that will not only add people to a list but will sort individuals based on different circumstances such as schedules and preferences.

What is Waitlist?

Collects company information and personalizes the waitlist options for your business.
